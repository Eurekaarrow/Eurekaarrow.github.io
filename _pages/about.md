## About me

I am a third-year undergraduate student currently pursuing a B.S. (Honours) in Computer Science and Technology at [Beijing Normal University - Hong Kong Baptist University United International College](https://www.uic.edu.cn/en/) (BNU-HKBU UIC). My research interests include generative AI, multi-modality and low-level computer vision.
